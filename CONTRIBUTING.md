# Contributing

## AI Collaboration

This repository is produced through collaboration between human contributors and AI tools.
All AI-assisted outputs should be reviewed, validated, and curated by maintainers before inclusion.

## Licensing of Contributions

By submitting a contribution to go-keylane, you agree that your contribution is
licensed under the same terms as the project:

```text
GPL-3.0-only
```

Do not submit code, documentation, generated files, test fixtures, vendored source,
or other materials unless you have the right to contribute them under these terms.

## Third-Party Materials

If a contribution adds, vendors, copies, generates from, or links to third-party
materials, include the relevant license and notice updates in the same pull
request. At minimum, update:

- `THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md`
- source headers or file-level notices when required by the upstream license

Do not replace upstream copyright or license headers with go-keylane headers.
Preserve the upstream notices and add modification notes where appropriate.

## Developer Certificate of Origin

Contributors are expected to certify that they have the right to submit their
contribution under the project license terms. A formal contributor license agreement
may be introduced before accepting broad external contributions.
